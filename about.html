About Us

JIH provides quality, prompt design & drafting. for all your needs.

Understanding clients' needs and delivering the best design solution is the fundamental principal upon which our business is based. No job is too big or small for us we undertake all kind of works. We deal in residential and commercial services.

JIH offers design & drafting service using latest & up to date software packages allowing for a interactive design process made up of 3D presentations, internal walkthroughs & colour renderings. With the rapid advancement of technology & computer aided drafting JIH aims to be at the forefront of the industry providing clients a service they deserve in this modern age. Maintaining & honest & prompt service is paramount in our business practices.



JIH provides a one stop shop where by services are not limited to design & drafting but include:

  • Lodgement of all plans & documentation to council / certifying authority.
  • Preparation/coordination of: BASIX certificates - Landscape plans - drainage plans - bushfire reports - structural engineering - construction certificate documentation etc.
  • Allowing JIH to source all necessary consultants results in prompt & stress free process. Our services can be tailored to suit varying requirement
